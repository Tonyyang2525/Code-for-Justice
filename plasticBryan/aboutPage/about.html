Introduction

Welcome to R2H GEO WILD, founded in 2014 with a vision to revolutionize the approach to recycling. Our mission is to unite frontline communities and organizations to drive a paradigm shift in the recycling movement. Through innovative strategies and mobilization efforts, we aim to champion a sustainable recycling ecosystem that minimizes waste and maximizes resource efficiency. At R2H GEO WILD, we are dedicated to promoting a circular economy where materials are continually reused and recycled, reducing the strain on natural resources and mitigating environmental impacts. We believe that equitable access to recycling opportunities is essential, and we work tirelessly to ensure that recycling initiatives are inclusive and accessible to all communities. Join us as we strive to build a future where recycling is not just a choice but a fundamental pillar of responsible stewardship of our planet.

Together, We

1) We inspire and mobilize communities at the forefront of environmental challenges to challenge the current linear economy that depletes resources and harms ecosystems.
2) We strive to cultivate resilient, regenerative, and equitable recycling systems rooted in local communities and their interconnected social and ecological networks.
3) We work to debunk misleading solutions to the recycling crisis, ensuring that resources are directed towards initiatives that do not exacerbate social or economic disparities or further damage the environment.
4) We confront governmental bodies and industries to take decisive action towards advancing recycling efforts and mitigating climate change impacts.

Together, our members have achieved significant victories in combating pollution and promoting sustainable recycling practices, effectively reducing carbon emissions. We are dedicated to fostering local alternatives that prioritize traditional ecological wisdom and cultural knowledge, paving the way for a future where recycling is a cornerstone of regenerative living. Join us in shaping a world where recycling isn't just a practice but a transformative force for positive change.

Membership Profile

Within R2H GEO WILD, we stand united as a diverse coalition comprising 59 urban and rural communities, organizations, and networks dedicated to advancing recycling and environmental justice. Our alliance is a vibrant tapestry of:

- Locally and regionally rooted racial and economic justice organizations representing Indigenous Peoples, Black, Latinx, Asian Pacific Islander, and economically disadvantaged white communities.

- Together, we share a history of enduring racial and economic injustices, and a commitment to grassroots social justice movements.

Our member organizations play a pivotal role in leading the charge for sustainable recycling practices and environmental equity. They spearhead impactful recycling initiatives that address the interconnected issues of social, racial, economic, and environmental justice. Through collaborative efforts, we aim to create inclusive and resilient recycling systems that uplift marginalized communities and promote a more just and sustainable future for all. Join us as we work hand in hand to champion recycling as a catalyst for positive social change and environmental stewardship.
